Frequently Asked Questions
  • How does it work?
    It's terribly simple: we collect the cryptos used to mint the Pigs inside the Pig's smart contract. Once all the 8888 pigs are minted, one will be drawn automatically. The owner of that Pig will be able to withdraw everything from the smart contract.
  • It is secure?
    The website code is fully available on github. Smart contracts can be audited on etherscanner. Once the lottery is started no one can stop it. Please check our smart contracts to find easter eggs and special functions!
  • What is the referral program?
    Share your link (lucky-pigs.github.io?friend=YOUR ADDRESS). For each pig minted trough this link you will receive a free pig, an opportunity to win 1M$!
  • What it I find a bug?
    If you are a black hat, enjoy your moneys! If you are a white hat, open an issue on the website github. We have 10 pigs assigned as bug bounties. Please notice that at this stage we are only giving bounties to bugs connected to severe security issues on the smart contracts. Bugs on the website or not exploitable bugs on the smart contracts are not going to be rewarded with pigs.
  • Can I chat with you?
    Sure! We are on twitter! And no, we are not on Discord!
  • When will the lottery finish?
    Once all the pigs are sold! That day, one pig will be sorted by the smart contract. The owner of that pig will win everything!
  • Can I re sell a pig?
    Sure! On Opensea or wherever you want! Please notice that selling a pig means to sell the capability to withdraw in case of win. So, buy a Pig today and sell it once the price raises!
  • How does the pricing works?
    The price raises while the pigs are sold following the table on this website. Buy a Pig today to obtain a good price.
